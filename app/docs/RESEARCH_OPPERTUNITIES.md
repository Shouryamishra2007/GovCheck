# Research Opportunities in GovCheck

## Publication-Ready Research Areas

### 1. **Opportunity Matching Algorithm**
- **Topic**: Collaborative filtering for career recommendations
- **Datasets**: GovCheck exam database + user interaction logs
- **Methods**: Matrix factorization, content-based filtering
- **Publication**: Recommend to: ACM RecSys, IJCAI

### 2. **NLP for Government Notification Parsing**
- **Topic**: Named Entity Recognition (NER) for Indian job notifications
- **Dataset Creation**: Annotated corpus of 500+ government notifications
- **Techniques**: Transformer models (BERT, mBERT), domain adaptation
- **Publication**: ACL, EMNLP

### 3. **Fairness in Career Recommendations**
- **Topic**: Bias detection and mitigation in educational opportunity recommenders
- **Research Question**: How do recommendation systems perpetuate category/gender bias?
- **Methods**: Fairness metrics (disparate impact), debiasing techniques
- **Publication**: FAccT (Fairness, Accountability, and Transparency)

### 4. **Career Trajectory Prediction**
- **Topic**: Time-series prediction of career paths
- **Data**: Student profiles → exam choices → career outcomes
- **Models**: RNNs, LSTMs, Graph Neural Networks
- **Publication**: IEEE TNNLS

### 5. **Educational Data Mining**
- **Topic**: Discover patterns in exam eligibility and educational requirements
- **Methods**: Association rule mining, clustering
- **Insights**: What education combinations lead to government careers?
- **Publication**: Educational Data Mining (EDM) Conference

## Implementation Roadmap

1. Collect anonymized user interaction data
2. Annotate 200+ government notifications for NER
3. Build baseline models (content-based filtering)
4. Experiment with advanced techniques
5. Publish preprint on arXiv
6. Submit to top-tier venues
